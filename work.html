Professional Work

I am currently a digital content manager with over ten years of experience in managing online content and adapting to new social media trends to engage our target audience. I am knowledgable in front-end development, which includes the basics of HTML, CSS, and JS. I enjoy designing and developing sites that are lightweight, simple, and responsive.

I have also worked heavily in digital and print content creation, designing anything from flyers, to posters, to program booklets, to stickers, to t-shirts. In addition to designing a new website for my own youth group, I worked on designing all of our advertising collaterals and our t-shirts and hoodies. I even developed a YouTube channel focused primarily on using graphics to teach and engage youth in Catholic Word of the Day.

My current employ also has me working in client-facing relations and cross-departmental team management. I have additional experience in leadership, organization, communication, public speaking, and event planning.
